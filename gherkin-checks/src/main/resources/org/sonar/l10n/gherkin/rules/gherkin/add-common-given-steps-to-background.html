To make the feature and its scenarios more readable, all Given steps common to all scenarios should be grouped in a Background.

Noncompliant Code Example

Feature: Cart management

  Scenario: As a customer, add a product to the cart
    Given I am a customer
    And I am on the catalog page
    When I add a product to my cart
    ...

    Scenario: As a customer, remove a product from the cart
      Given I am a customer
      And I have products in my cart
      When I remove a product from my cart
      ...

Compliant Solution

Feature: Cart management

  Background:
    Given I am a customer

  Scenario: As a customer, add a product to the cart
    Given I am on the catalog page
    When I add a product to my cart
    ...

    Scenario: As a customer, remove a product from the cart
      Given I have products in my cart
      When I remove a product from my cart
      ...
